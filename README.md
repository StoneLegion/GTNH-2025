## Let's Play Goals:
- Taking our time with the pack. For example we might do every bronze multi block possible before moving on LV.
- Try to do as many quests as enjoyable as possible before moving onto other tiers. This is more like a take our time, smell the roses, don't just rush through a tier and ignore most of the quests. 
- Build big, Build silly, have fun. There are no rules besides no cheating. I need to learn to have fun not have the voice on my shoulder telling me I can't do this and that or that is cheating, etc. Play the back and have fun!
- Trying our best to make it to Mars and maybe even Beyond!
